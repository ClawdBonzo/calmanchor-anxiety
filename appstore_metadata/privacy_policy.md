# GW Labs Privacy Policy

**Effective date: May 31, 2026**

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile applications published by GW Labs ("we," "us," "our") — collectively, "our apps." We design our apps to be private by default. This policy explains what our apps do and do not do with your information. Where a specific app's behavior differs from what is described here, the disclosures shown on that app's App Store product page ("App Privacy" label) apply to that app.

## The short version

- Content you create in our apps is stored **only on your device** unless an app explicitly states otherwise. We do not receive it and do not upload it to any account or cloud we control.
- Our apps generally have **no user accounts and no login**. You are not required to give us your name, email, or any identity to use them.
- We use **no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs**, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.
- The only information that leaves your device is what is needed to process and validate an optional purchase or subscription, as described below.

## Information stored on your device

Content you create in our apps — such as entries, logs, history, preferences, and settings — is stored in local storage on your device. This data is not transmitted to us and is not backed up to any account we control. If you delete an app, its data is removed with it, subject to any device-level backups you control through Apple (such as iCloud Backup), which are governed by Apple's terms.

## Purchases and subscriptions

Some of our apps offer optional in-app purchases and subscriptions. To process, validate, and restore these purchases, we use:

- **Apple / App Store** — Apple processes all payments. We never see or receive your payment-card details. Apple's handling of your data is governed by Apple's privacy policy.
- **RevenueCat** — our subscription-management provider. To verify your purchase status across reinstalls and devices, RevenueCat receives a **randomly generated, anonymous app-user identifier** and **purchase/transaction data** (such as which product was bought and its status). This identifier is not your name, email, or Apple ID. It is used only to deliver the purchase functionality you requested.

We do not use this information for advertising, and we do not sell it. RevenueCat's processing of this data is governed by RevenueCat's privacy policy.

## Data we "collect" (App Store privacy disclosure)

For apps that offer purchases, the only data associated with you through the providers above is:

- **Purchase History** — used for App Functionality (managing your purchase or subscription).
- **A device-generated identifier** — used for App Functionality (validating your purchase). Not linked to your identity and not used to track you.

Unless an individual app's App Store privacy label states otherwise, we do not collect your contact info, your in-app content, your location, your contacts, or your browsing/search history.

## Children's privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because our apps generally have no accounts and store content on-device, no personal information is gathered from any user, including minors.

## Your choices and rights

Because your content lives on your device, you remain in control of it. You can view, edit, or delete your content within the app, remove all app data by deleting the app, and manage or cancel any subscription through your Apple ID subscription settings. Depending on where you live (for example, under the EU/UK GDPR or the California CCPA), you may have additional rights regarding personal data. Since we do not hold your in-app content on our servers, most such requests can be fulfilled directly on your device; for anything involving purchase records, contact us using the details below.

## Health and wellness apps

Some of our apps provide self-help or wellness tools. Such apps are not medical devices and are not a substitute for professional diagnosis, treatment, or emergency care. If you are in crisis, contact the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988 in the US), text HOME to 741741, or call 911 or your local emergency number.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective date" above. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
